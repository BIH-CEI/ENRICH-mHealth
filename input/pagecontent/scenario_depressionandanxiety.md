This scenario shows how ENRICH-mHealth represents the GRID HAMD 17 Questionnaire used for remote assessment of depression and anxiety severity.

> **Note:** The electronic consent profiles (Forschungsvorhaben / Videosprechstunde) inherited from the UTN guide are currently not part of the build, because their MII Consent / Einwilligungsmanagement base packages ship without snapshots. They are retained under `_disabled-fsh/` and will be re-enabled once snapshot-bearing dependency versions are available.

### GRID HAMD 17 - Questionnaire
The LOINC Codes are requested and currently in WIP. Until the codes are published, the codesystem for the questionnaire is created.
